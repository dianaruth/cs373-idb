Data Sources

www.swapi.co

We used the Star Wars API, or swapi, to scrape basic data about each person, planet, and species. Swapi gives us miscellaneous information such as climate, hair color, height, classification, gravity, and other fields that are useful when describing a person, planet, or species.

Wookieepedia

We used Wookieepedia to manually gather images and descriptions for each person, planet, and species we documented. These images and descriptions are loaded on the detail pages.
